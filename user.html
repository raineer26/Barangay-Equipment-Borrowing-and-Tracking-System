ABOUT THE BARANGAY

Barangay Mapulang Lupa in Valenzuela City is a vibrant and growing community that takes pride in its unity, service, and continuous progress. With dedicated leaders and active citizens, the barangay provides essential services such as public safety, health programs, community activities, and facilities that support the needs of every resident.

More than just a place to live, Mapulang Lupa is a community where people work hand in hand, celebrate milestones together, and strive to build a stronger and better future.
