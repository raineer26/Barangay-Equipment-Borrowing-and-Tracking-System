Once upon a time before the year of 1950, Barangay Mapulang Lupa and Barangay Ugong, are one and the same andn they called of Barrio Ugong. The place have many trees, pure virgin forest, stony like a simple mountain that you can always see in every place in this Barrio. The stony part of the place is wider than farms. In the place were Mapulang Lupa situated, their on 17 houses or households who lives in this place. This 17 families though that their place was already abandoned and because of this situation, they unite to build and construct a "mini chaphel" where they used this when the time of holy week(Semana Santa) and celebration of fiesta.

During that time the place was in demand for "PATIBAGAN NG BATO" and they called it "Eskombro" in every excavation they did, in deepest part , they always found red soil "PULANG LUPA" even in the farm and place where they plant some vegetables and others they always saw a "Pulang Lupa" or Red Soil.

And when they say it to the people of Ugong the eagerness and ardent desire to seperate the place and they called it "MAPULANG LUPA" until the Sangguniang Bayan of Polo granted in 1950.
