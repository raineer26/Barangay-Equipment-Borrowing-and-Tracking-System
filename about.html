ABOUT US

Learn more about Barangay Mapulang Lupa

Barangay Mapulang Lupa in Valenzuela City is a vibrant and growing community that takes pride in its unity, service, and continuous progress. With dedicated leaders and active citizens, the barangay provides essential services such as public safety, health programs, community activities, and facilities that support the needs of every resident.

More than just a place to live, Mapulang Lupa is a community where people work hand in hand, celebrate milestones together, and strive to build a stronger and better future.

Mission

To provide efficient and responsive public services that promote the welfare, safety, and development of our community through transparent governance and active citizen participation.

The barangay commits to continuous improvement of services, capacity building for staff, and strengthening partnerships with civil society to better serve the residents.

Vision

A progressive, peaceful, and united barangay where every resident enjoys quality services, sustainable development, and an improved quality of life.

We envision a community that embraces inclusive growth, environmental stewardship, and opportunities for all generations to thrive.
